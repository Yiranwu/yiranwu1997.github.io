dating profile, but make it human

Hi, I'm Yiran.

I like thoughtful conversation, emotional clarity, playful curiosity, and people who can be sincere without taking themselves too seriously.

If we end up dating, I hope it feels like building a small world together: warm, funny, communicative, and a little adventurous.

Snapshot

  • good listener
  • curious mind
  • likes museums and long walks
  • values direct communication
  • soft spot for great food
  • romantic, but not chaotic

I tend to connect best with people who...

are kind under stress, ask real questions, and can talk honestly about what they want.

I’m drawn to people who are intellectually alive, emotionally grounded, and excited to make ordinary days feel a bit more special.

What dating me feels like

Thoughtful planning, affectionate check-ins, and a genuine interest in who you are beyond the surface.

I care a lot about mutual effort. I like momentum, but I don’t like games.

Green flags that win me over

  • You communicate clearly instead of hinting and hoping.
  • You are warm to service staff, strangers, and your friends.
  • You have your own passions and love talking about them.
  • You can laugh easily, especially at yourself.
  • You want something real, not vague.

An ideal first date

Start somewhere cozy with great coffee or wine, wander into a bookstore, art space, or neighborhood neither of us has properly explored, then keep talking until one of us realizes we forgot to check the time.

A few things I’m into lately

Conversations

The kind that move easily between life plans, small embarrassments, and oddly specific opinions.

Places

Quiet cafes, beautiful grocery stores, city walks at golden hour, and anywhere with unexpectedly good dessert.

Energy

Calm confidence, reciprocal interest, and someone who is comfortable being both tender and funny.
